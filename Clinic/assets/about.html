Dr Hany Abdel Gawwad

With extensive experience in treating scoliosis, kyphosis, congenital spinal deformities, and complex revision spine surgery, Professor Abdel Gawwad is particularly known for managing cases that are often considered high-risk or inoperable. His work has transformed the lives of thousands of patients suffering from severe, neglected, or complex spinal conditions—restoring not only spinal alignment, but also confidence, mobility, and overall quality of life. Professor Abdel Gawwad completed advanced fellowship training in adult and pediatric spine surgery at the University of Montreal, Canada, where he developed deep expertise in modern deformity correction techniques, spinal osteotomies, and advanced surgical strategies. He currently serves as Professor of Orthopedic Surgery at the Al-Azhar University, Cairo, and practices across leading medical centers in Egypt, the United Arab Emirates, and Kuwait.

Renowned for combining scientific precision with genuine human compassion, Professor Abdel Gawwad adopts a patient-centered approach, carefully tailoring each treatment plan to the individual patient’s anatomy, age, functional needs, and life goals. His surgical philosophy emphasizes maximum correction with maximum safety, supported by state-of-the-art technology and meticulous preoperative planning for every single patient.

His work has earned him widespread recognition among both patients and medical professionals, and he is often described as a surgeon who restores hope where others see limits.

This website is dedicated to sharing his vision, expertise, and outcomes in spine deformity correction—offering patients and families a trusted destination for knowledge, care, and transformation.
